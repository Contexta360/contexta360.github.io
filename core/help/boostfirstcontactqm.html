The First Contact Resolution metric is one of the top KPIs
for customer experience because it looks at both efficiency and effectiveness of how your contact centre is performing.
First Contact Resolution is essential for a business when managing relationships with customers. It is a reflection on the contact centre is staffed with trained agents, and their capacity to resolve queries, without the need to transfer or escalate the call. Companies with high first call resolution scores see higher customer satisfaction scores, as well as being able to reduce operating costs and higher customer and employee satisfaction.

This metric creates a query to detect when calls are not resolved. One example is a case where the agent apologises at the end of the conversation, or states that they have had to call in again. Conversations without First Call Resolution will show the terms that indicate the issue that they were calling in for has not been resolved.
