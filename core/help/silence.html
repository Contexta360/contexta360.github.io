Silence detection enables you to detect configurable amounts of silence in conversations.
This metric will analyse the number of calls that contain at least one silence of longer than 10 seconds.
Silence detection can help identify the agents that have frequent and significant pauses or 'non %E2%80%93 talk time' speaking to customers and, identify agents that talk-over customers. Agents can be provided with additional training and support to improve their performance and productivity, which can help identify key agent and customer behaviour.

The measurement is in seconds, and by reducing the amount of silence, this metric can be used to highlight the need for training or can help identifying skill gaps, that can help increase agent productivity.
