Manage Metrics
Understand when you callers are mentioning your competitors. Are they looking to churn, or perhaps there are better deals or offers?

The user can provide a list of competitors. When competitors are mentioned, they are displayed as an event on the timeline.
This metric enables users to highlight when a competitor is mentioned, so that they can uncover insights on how competitive their service, price or solutions are and whether callers are mentioning these competitors, with a view to drive commercial requirements, such as price, discount, shipping etc, or to be more cognisant about potential risks such as churn and/or other factors.
