General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is the most recent regulation that contact centres need to comply with in Europe.

GDPR applies to any business that accepts and stores information for EU residents even if the business is not located in Europe. The purpose of the GDPR is to grant ownership of sensitive information to individuals. They can request all stored data and for it to be erased.
