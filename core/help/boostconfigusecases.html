Description
This page enables you to manage the four pre-templated use cases, or add your own.
By clicking on the 'Configure' buttons, you will be take to the pre-templated metrics associated with the specific use case.
By clicking on the 'Create new use case' button, you will be taken to the page to enable you to create your own use cases.
A maximum of 10 use cases can be created. Any 4 can be displayed on the status monitor page at any one time.
