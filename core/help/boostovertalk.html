Understanding Overtalk is an important in being able to provide good customer satisfaction. This will be the percentage of the time that people talk at the same. Only possible with dual channel recordings.

In order for first call resolution to occur, the agent must be a good listener that lets the customer fully share their opinions. However, during these natural conversations, things do not play out perfectly the way scripts do, and people interrupt one another when speaking.
When more than one person speaks at the same time during a conversation, it is called Overtalk.

This is can be a problem for recording calls, as multiple people speaking at the same time distorts the audio. When it comes to speech-to-text, the software can have difficulty picking apart the different speakers.

Understanding why you are getting high Overtalk can help identify agent and customer frustrations and is a key indicator that if the caller and the agent are both talking over each other, neither one of them is probably listening. This could highlight lack of understanding, sentiment, and/or training.

Understanding the root cause will enable you to handle calls more efficiently whilst increasing customer satisfaction.
